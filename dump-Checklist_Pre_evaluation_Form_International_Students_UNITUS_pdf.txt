Checklist for Completing the Pre‑evaluation and Admission Interview
Form
This checklist is designed to support prospective international students in preparing all the
required information and documents before completing the pre‑evaluation and admission
interview booking form. Carefully following all the steps below significantly increases the
likelihood of a correct Universitaly pre‑enrolment and a successful admission process.
1. Personal Information (to be prepared in advance)
☐ Active personal email address (regularly checked)
☐ First name and family name exactly as shown on the passport
☐ Date and place of birth
☐ Gender (optional)
☐ Nationality (as stated on the passport)
2. Identity Document
☐ Valid passport
☐ Passport expiry date later than the expected enrolment date
☐ Clear and readable PDF file (page with photo and personal details)
☐ File size below 10 MB
3. Entry Qualification (Bachelor’s Degree)
☐ Official Bachelor’s degree certificate
☐ The degree grants access to a Master’s degree in the awarding country
☐ PDF format
☐ Official translation into English or Italian if the original document is issued in another
language
4. Transcript of Records
☐ Official transcript with a complete list of passed exams
☐ Clear indication of GPA/CGPA
☐ Indication of the grading scale used by the awarding institution
☐ PDF format
☐ Official translation if required
5. English Language Requirement (B2 level)
☐ English language certificate at minimum B2 level (IELTS, TOEFL, Cambridge, etc.)
or
☐ Official statement from the awarding university confirming that the Bachelor’s degree

-- 1 of 2 --

was fully taught in English
☐ PDF format
6. Curriculum Vitae (CV)
☐ Updated Curriculum Vitae
☐ Written in English
☐ Preferably in Europass format
☐ PDF format
7. Additional Academic Information
☐ Year of Bachelor’s degree graduation
☐ Awarding university
☐ Numerical GPA/CGPA
☐ Minimum and maximum values of the grading scale
8. Admission Interview Booking
☐ Check availability for the selected interview date in advance
☐ Book the interview at least 10 days before the chosen date
☐ Availability to attend an online interview (Zoom/Google Meet)
☐ Regularly check email to receive the interview link
9. Declarations and Awareness
☐ Read and understand the privacy notice (GDPR)
☐ Awareness that pre‑evaluation does not automatically guarantee admission
☐ Understanding that incomplete or incorrect documentation may lead to rejection
☐ Willingness to sign the Universitaly Readiness Self‑Declaration
10. Final Check Before Submission
☐ All mandatory fields in the form are completed
☐ All required documents are correctly uploaded
☐ Documents are clear, complete, and readable
☐ Information provided is consistent with the uploaded documents
☐ Confirmation email received after form submission
Note: Applicants are strongly advised to keep copies of all uploaded documents, as they will
also be required for the Universitaly pre‑enrolment procedure and for the student visa
application.
