nn Sample 10-Day Tourist Itinerary for the United States (For DS-160 Visa Application)
Day 1 – Arrival in New York City
- Arrive at JFK International Airport.
- Transfer to hotel in Manhattan.
- Rest or take a light evening walk around Times Square.
Day 2 – New York City
- Visit the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island.
- Explore Wall Street and the 9/11 Memorial.
- Walk across the Brooklyn Bridge.
Day 3 – New York City
- Visit the Metropolitan Museum of Art or the Museum of Natural History.
- Explore Central Park and Fifth Avenue.
- Dinner near Broadway.
Day 4 – Travel to Washington, D.C.
- Travel by train or plane to Washington, D.C.
- Visit the White House (from outside) and walk through the National Mall.
Day 5 – Washington, D.C.
- Visit the U.S. Capitol, Lincoln Memorial, and WWII Memorial.
- Explore a Smithsonian Museum (Air & Space or Natural History).
- Relax at the National Mall.
Day 6 – Flight to Orlando, Florida
- Domestic flight to Orlando, FL.
- Check-in at hotel.
- Explore International Drive in the evening.
Day 7 – Orlando: Theme Park Day
- Spend the day at Walt Disney World or Universal Studios Orlando.
Day 8 – Orlando: Relaxation
- Shopping at Florida Mall or outlet centers.
- Enjoy hotel amenities or attend an evening show.
Day 9 – Return to New York City
- Fly back to New York City for final day of trip.
- Free time or last-minute shopping.
Day 10 – Departure
- Transfer to JFK or Newark Airport for departure.
Notes for DS-160:
- U.S. Address: Use your hotel’s address in New York (e.g., Marriott Times Square or similar).
- Purpose of Trip: Tourism – No U.S. contacts (if no one is hosting you).
- Keep this travel plan consistent with what you enter on the DS-160 to avoid discrepancies.
- Cities can be adjusted depending on your preference (e.g., swap Orlando for Miami or Los Angeles).
