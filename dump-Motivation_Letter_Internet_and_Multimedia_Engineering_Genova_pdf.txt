Statement of Purpose
Master’s Degree in Internet and Multimedia Engineering
University of Genoa
Academic excellence, intellectual curiosity, and a strong commitment to interdisciplinary learning
have consistently defined my university trajectory. Ranked first in my graduating class (Major de
Promotion) in Information and Communication Sciences, I am applying to the Master’s programme
in Internet and Multimedia Engineering at the University of Genoa with the ambition to extend my
academic achievements into a rigorous and technology-driven field.
I obtained my Bachelor’s Degree (Licence) in Information and Communication Sciences from the
University of Batna 1, Algeria. Throughout my undergraduate studies, I maintained the highest
academic performance in my cohort, reflecting my dedication, discipline, and analytical rigor. My
academic training focused on communication theories, media systems, information circulation, and
the societal impact of digital technologies, allowing me to understand communication as a
structured and technologically mediated process.
As my studies progressed, my interest increasingly shifted toward the technical foundations of
digital communication. I became particularly interested in how internet architectures, multimedia
platforms, and interactive systems are designed and optimized. This intellectual evolution motivated
my decision to pursue an engineering-oriented programme that would allow me to acquire concrete
technical competencies alongside my theoretical background.
The Master’s programme in Internet and Multimedia Engineering at the University of Genoa
represents the ideal academic environment for this transition. Its focus on computer networks,
multimedia systems, and internet technologies aligns perfectly with my objective to combine a
human-centered understanding of communication with engineering expertise. My background in
communication sciences offers a valuable user-oriented perspective that complements technical
system design.
My long-term goal is to contribute to the development of innovative digital communication systems
and multimedia technologies, either in industry or research-oriented environments. I am confident
that my academic excellence, motivation, and interdisciplinary profile will allow me to successfully
meet the challenges of this programme.
Meriem Toumi
