Motivation Letter
Dear Members of the Admissions Committee,
I am writing to express my strong interest in joining the Master's Programme in
Disaster and Health Crisis Management (DHCM) at the Università del Piemonte
Orientale. I am motivated to continue my studies in this field because I am interested
in understanding how institutions and professionals respond to complex crises and
global challenges.
I hold a Bachelor's degree in Economics and Business Management. During my
studies, I developed knowledge in economic analysis, management, and decision-
making processes. These studies helped me understand how organizations operate
and how strategic decisions are taken in challenging situations.
Last year, I had the opportunity to receive an acceptance for this programme. I was
very happy and fully prepared to start my studies. Unfortunately, I was unable to
complete the visa process due to the unavailability of visa appointments at the Italian
embassy during that period. Despite several attempts, I could not obtain an
appointment before the deadline.
Even though I could not start the programme last year, my motivation to join the
DHCM programme has remained strong. This experience strengthened my
determination to pursue international postgraduate studies and to prepare myself for
the academic and professional challenges related to crisis management.
What attracts me most to the DHCM programme is its international and
multidisciplinary approach. I believe that managing disasters and health crises
requires cooperation between different fields and professionals. The opportunity to
learn from experts and collaborate with students from different backgrounds makes
this programme particularly valuable.
I am especially interested in studying at the Università del Piemonte Orientale
because of its recognized academic environment and its commitment to training
professionals capable of responding to complex global crises. I would be honored to
contribute positively to the academic community of the university and to represent my
country with dedication and seriousness during my studies.
My goal is to build a professional career where I can contribute to crisis management
and international cooperation. I believe that this programme will help me acquire the
knowledge and experience necessary to achieve this objective.
Thank you very much for considering my application.
Yours sincerely,
Menzer Abdelmalek.
