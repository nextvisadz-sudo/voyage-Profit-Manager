Motivation Letter
Dear Members of the Admissions Committee,
I am writing to express my strong interest in the Master’s Degree in Economics and Public Policy at
the University of Bologna. With a solid academic background in finance and a strong interest in
economic analysis and institutional frameworks, I am eager to further develop my knowledge in
public economics and policy evaluation within a highly respected and academically diverse
environment.
I hold a Bachelor’s and a Master’s degree in Finance from the University of Batna 1. Throughout my
studies, I acquired strong foundations in microeconomics, macroeconomics, financial accounting,
corporate finance, investment analysis, taxation, auditing, and risk management. My academic
training strengthened my analytical and quantitative skills, particularly in financial analysis,
budgeting, and performance evaluation. I developed the ability to interpret data, assess economic
situations, and understand how financial and economic decisions influence organizations and
markets.
In addition to my university education, I completed vocational training in Office Secretariat, Data
Entry, and Statistical Assistance, which enhanced my practical skills in data management,
reporting, and administrative organization. These experiences allowed me to gain hands-on
experience in database management, document preparation, and statistical reporting —
competencies that are directly relevant to policy analysis and evidence-based decision-making.
My professional experience as an Administrative Assistant further strengthened my organizational
skills and sense of responsibility. I was involved in data entry, report preparation, coordination
between departments, and administrative planning. Working in this role gave me insight into how
regulations, administrative procedures, and economic decisions interact within institutions. It also
reinforced my discipline, precision, and ability to meet deadlines.
I am particularly attracted to the Economics and Public Policy program because it combines
rigorous quantitative economic analysis with the study of public institutions and policy design. I am
motivated to understand how governments and public organizations develop policies, evaluate their
impact, and address economic and social challenges through evidence-based strategies.
Moreover, I am especially interested in studying at the University of Bologna due to its strong
academic tradition and the breadth of its programs in economics. The university’s diverse
specializations and interdisciplinary approach create an intellectually stimulating environment that
encourages academic growth and international perspective — qualities that I value deeply in my
educational journey.
My long-term objective is to work in economic policy analysis, public administration, or international
institutions where I can contribute to designing and evaluating policies that promote sustainable
development and social welfare. I am serious, organized, adaptable, and fully committed to
academic excellence. I am confident that my background, motivation, and work ethic will allow me
to succeed in your program and actively contribute to your academic community.
Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to the opportunity to join the University of
Bologna and continue developing my academic and professional path.
