Please note that the following are NOT allowed: dictionary and/or thesaurus, formula lists, concept
maps, periodic table of elements, personal computer, smartphone, and tablet.
If for logistic reasons and/or binding dispositions it is not possible to guarantee the requested aids
it will be offered alternative equivalent measures.
ATTENTION: To the request you have to attach health documents attesting the need for adaptation, issues
in the country of residence. The documents have to be in Italian or English, or in original language and
accompanied by a certified translation in Italian or English.
Any request without attachments will not be considered.
Valid health documents are:
- Diagnosis of Specific learning difficulties (SLD)
- Certificate of handicap;
- Certificate of legal disability;
- Documentation confirming the existence of an illness that might result in an inability to study,
temporary or otherwise1;
- Other documentation attesting a Learning disability (LD);
1 Reference is made to health conditions that might result in certain specific needs arising during the test.
